Philosophy

I bridge the worlds of art and technology by creating visuals and digital experiences that feel both precise and deeply expressive. With a fine arts background, I bring a painter’s eye for detail, and through my frontend development journey, I cultivate a strong appreciation for clean structure, tidy logic, and numerical precision.

I combine the emotional depth of oil painting, the visual storytelling of graphic design, and the organized, methodical nature of frontend development to create work that is balanced, thoughtful, and impactful.

I craft designs and digital experiences that are not only beautiful, but also functional, intuitive, and built with intention.
